Working with Page Views and Contents

This chapter explains how to display page views and modify page contents. A page view is the area of the Acrobat or Acrobat Reader window that displays the visible content of a document page. An example of a page view is a PDF document page displayed within Acrobat Reader or Acrobat at a 120% magnification.
