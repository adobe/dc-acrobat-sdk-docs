What Adobe resources are recommended to Acrobat developers?

For plug-ins and applications available from Adobe or third parties, Acrobat/PDF service providers, or links to pages on how to use the available plug-ins, see the Acrobat Developer Center.

For white papers and case studies of what can be done and what has been done with Acrobat, see http://www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/main.html.
