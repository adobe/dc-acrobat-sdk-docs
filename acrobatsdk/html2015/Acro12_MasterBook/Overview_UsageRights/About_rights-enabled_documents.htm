About rights-enabled documents

When creating a PDF document, it is possible to assign it special rights that enable users of Acrobat Reader to fill in forms, participate in online reviews, and attach files. LiveCycle ReaderÂ® Extensions can be used to activate additional functionality within Acrobat Reader for a particular document, thereby enabling the user to save, fill in, annotate, sign, certify, authenticate, and submit the document. This streamlines collaboration for document reviews and the automating of data capture with electronic forms. Users of Acrobat Professional DC can also Reader-enable collaboration. You cannot programmatically rights-enable a PDF file.
